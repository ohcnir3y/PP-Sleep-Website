Sleep Tracker

Now that you know more about sleep and the importance of good sleep quality, you need a tool to record your sleep. A sleep diary is a convenient and useful way to track sleep at home. You should aim to record your sleep for at least two weeks, and it's best if you complete it daily. The diary allows you to record when you went to bed, whether you woke up during the night, and when you woke up in the morning. Ultimately, it can help you understand your sleep pattern and improve it. If you notice a pattern, you can make one change at a time and maintain it that way for around two weeks. Eventually, you will be able to maintain good sleep quality.

The following sleep diary was specifically designed for teenagers and takes into account various factors that can hinder adolescents' sleep.
