Sleep Biology

Many people wrongly believe that sleep is a simple process–you go to bed, then you fall asleep. However, this is not quite accurate as sleep is governed by biology, not simple magic. In fact, the body relies on internal systems such as the circadian rhythm and homeostatic sleep drive to go to sleep. And even sleep itself is not one concept–it is divided into different stages, each characterized by different reactions from our body.
