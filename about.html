About Sleep Smart

Did you know that most teenagers don't get enough sleep? Lack of sleep can affect your mood, focus, and overall performance. I created this website to help students understand the biology behind sleep, and ultimately its importance.

Why I Made this Project

I started this project because I struggled with consistent sleep myself. As I transitioned to high school, I noticed many students around me found regular sleep schedules challenging to maintain as well. I wanted to understand why sleep was important and how teen sleep is different. Through the project, I learned that small changes can improve our energy and focus.

What this Website Offers

  • On this website, you'll find:
    • Content summarized from multiple research papers and overviews
    • Practical tips to improve your sleep
    • A 5-day sleep tracker to help you monitor sleep quality
    • Fun Quizzes to test your sleep knowledge
    • Insights from research on teen sleep patterns

Note from Founder

Whether you're trying to get more consistent sleep or curious about sleep biology, this website will prove valuable to you. It will help you understand your sleep patterns and general teen sleep. I sincerely hope this website helps you make improvements that can have a big impact on your day-to-day life.
